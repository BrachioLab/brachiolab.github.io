Welcome to Brachio Lab.

Our research focuses on developing techniques that enable reliable and explainable machine learning systems in a verifiable or provable way. We work closely with cosmologists and surgeons to ensure that our algorithms produce results and guarantees that are understandable and usable by experts.

News: We are at NeurIPS! Come find us at Poster #1625 on Tuesday, Dec 12 at 10:45AM CST to chat with Anton about stability guarantees for feature attributions. Shreya, Weiqiu, and Anton will also be at the XAI in Action workshop to talk about this and other explainability work on faithful attributions and visual topics.
